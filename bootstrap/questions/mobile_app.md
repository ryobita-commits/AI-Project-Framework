# Initial Questions: Mobile App

1. What app are you trying to create?
2. Who is the target user?
3. What is the core user journey?
4. What is the MVP?
5. Which platform comes first: iOS, Android, or both?
6. Are there backend or sync requirements?
7. Are notifications, subscriptions, or app-store constraints relevant?
8. What is out of scope initially?
9. What metrics define success?
10. What decisions are still open?
