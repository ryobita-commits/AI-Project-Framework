# Initial Questions: Sales Plan

1. What market or segment is the plan for?
2. What revenue or pipeline outcome is being targeted?
3. Who are the buyers or accounts?
4. What channels or motions are in scope?
5. What time horizon matters?
6. What constraints exist around budget, team, or territory?
7. What data already exists?
8. What is out of scope?
9. How will success be measured?
10. What decisions remain open?
